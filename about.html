Welcome to Izyan Mini Mart

Background

Izyan Mini Mart was established in November 2023 and was a student-centric retail business designed to meet the dynamic needs of the campus community. It serves as a convenient hub, offering a wide range of essential products, including groceries, snacks, beverages, medicines and personal care items, as well as stationeries. With affordability and accessibility at its core, the store aims to provide a seamless shopping experience for students, faculty, and staff. Tailored to the fast-paced lifestyle of young adults, the mini supermarket emphasizes quick service, trendy product selections, and value-added offerings such as grab-and-go meals and late-night essentials. By creating a welcoming atmosphere and offering products that suit the diverse needs of the campus community, the mini mart becomes an important part of students' and staff's daily lives.

Logo


  • Orange: Orange symbolizes enthusiasm, creativity, and friendliness. It conveys a sense of warmth and approachability, making customers feel welcome. In the context of a mini mart, it suggests a vibrant, student-friendly place where needs are met efficiently.

  • Black: Black continues to symbolize strength, reliability, and professionalism, reassuring customers of quality service and trustworthiness.

  • White: White represents simplicity and cleanliness, creating a fresh and inviting look for the logo, aligning with the expectations of a modern and organized mini mart.

Vision

To be the leading convenience store that seamlessly integrates into campus life, enhancing the university experience through exceptional service, community engagement, and sustainable practices.

Mission

To be the main convenience store for the university community, providing easy access to good quality products that meet everyday needs, encourage connections on campus, and improve the well-being of students, teachers, and staff.

Objectives

  • To provide easily accessible and convenient shopping options for students, faculty, and staff, ensuring they have access to essential products close to campus.

  • To prioritize excellent customer service, ensuring a positive and efficient shopping experience.

  • To foster a connection with the campus community by supporting events, promotions, and collaborations with student organizations.
